I WORLDWIDE MEETING

About the Event

The chosen location was CATRE - Centro Adventista de Treinamento e Recreação, in Palmas – Santa Catarina, very near Florianopolis in south of Brazil. CATRE has one of the best infrastructures of the south of the country, with beautiful leisure areas and paradisiac views when we are going to live the best moments of this meeting. We are preparing a lot of surprises that will make your mark on AMM, besides being a unique opportunity to get to know the members of this worldwide family.

Registration fee: was BRL 120,00 is BRL 100,00

Discount for a limited time for registrations until March 31st, 2019. Vacancies are limited to 470 seats which is the maximum place accommodation.

USEFUL INFORMATIONS

Accomodation

We recommend your accomodation at CATRE, the official place where all the scheduling and activities of the I AMM Worldwide Meeting will happen.

CATRE's cottage is equipped with private room for couple or 2 singles with box bed, TV, air conditioning in the room, sink, stove, refrigerator, ceiling fan in the living room, 1 sofa bed in the living room with capacity for 2 people.

Acommodation costs

  • Cottage: BRL 492,00 (three meals included, reserved for Americans)
The cost refers to checking in on 11/15/2019 at 1p.m, checking out on 11/18/2019 at 9a.m, including three meals per day and all the infrastructure as well as the leisure areas.

Children from 10 years of age pay the full amount, while 5 to 9 years old have 50% discount, and 0 to 4 years do not pay.

After 5 years of age, the child must be registered with the members in the registration system for bed reservation purposes. Therefore, you will be charged the full value of the registration.

The room bookings should be made by the state director directly with CATRE, by phone. It will be necessary to inform the provided number when applying for your registration in the event. When booking, at least 15% of the accommodation fee should be paid and the final payment can be done at the moment you check in at the event. It can also be paid by credit card, divided into up to 10 portions (the minimum portion is BRL 200,00).

About the infrastructure: a perfect place with heated and natural child and adult swimming pools, aquatic sports pool, dry and wet sauna, Swiss and court soccer, basketball court, tennis, beach tennis and beach volleyball sand court. There are bocce playing ground, tetherball and a brand new facility: game room with table soccer, table tennis, snooker, convenience room and gym. Also, lots of spots to hang a hammock, a resting deck to read, listen to the birds singing, and a bicycle rack available with no additional costs.

Check out CATRE website for more information and photos. www.catre.org.br

"I am not staying at CATRE"

This is one of the option you can choose. Still, it is possible to use CATRE services by paying for them separately.

Day Use - BRL 25,00 this fee will be charged only for those who wish to spend the day enjoying CATRE leisure infrastructure. Do not want, do not pay (there will be supervision).

Food – BRL 78,00: this is the price for all three meals at CATRE, or you can opt to have individual meals:

  • Breakfast – BRL 25,00
  • Lunch – BRL 28,00
  • Dinner – BRL 25,00

If you prefer to camp, there is a camping area in front of CATRE that will be available for BRL 40,00 per person. In this case, contact directly the owner to check vacancies at Camping Palmas das Gaivotas +55 (48) 99122-4362 – talk with Mr. Alcides.

Material Sales

The chapters can sell items, such as: stickers, pins, t-shirts, caps and other products. This should be informed at the moment of the registration. Individual member´s sales will not be allowed.

Ride To Serra do Rio Rastro and Others (Optional)

Our event ends on Sunday, 17th, at night and the accomodation ends the next day at 8a.m with the breakfast. A ride will be offered to Serra do Rio do Rastro, Pedra Furada, etc, nevertheless, each one is free to do it or not.

If you would like to have lunch at the restaurant during the ride on 11/18 at Serra do Rio do Rastro, we are in contact with the barbecue house Churrascaria Cascata. The house is closed on Mondays, but if there are a lot of people interested in this option, it can open for us. The price is BRL 55,00 all-you-can-eat barbecue and BRL 39,50 all-you-can-eat buffet (today prices). If you would like to join it you should the specific field on your registration form.

Our responsability as organizers of the event ends at the Serra do Rio do Rastro and Pedra Furada ride. Whoever wants to participate in other rides can organize groups to do so.
